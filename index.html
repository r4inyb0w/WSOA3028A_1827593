About Me

Hi there! Thanks for clicking on my website.

My name is Macey Daniels. I am 22-years-old and I run a florist call Daniels' Daisies. You should check it out! My mom passed the family business on to me when I graduated from the University of the Witwatersrand, with my own business degree. I love flowers and all their different meanings. My favourite flower is the Iris - it represents hope. You can explore more about flowers and my floral arrangements on this site. I'm also using this site to spread awareness of some amazing things happening around South Africa. Check out my beliefs page to link to cool articles and see some interesting streetart I've come across.
