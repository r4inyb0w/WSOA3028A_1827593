A little example of the digital divide

Friday, 15 May

A screenshot of a Discord video chat

During lockdown, the best way for me to hang out with my friends is over Discord. I acknowledge that this in itself is an example of privileged access, considering one needs a capable computer and a sizeable amount of data to join into this conversation. This is an example of the first stage of the digital divide, "the haves and have nots".

However, this screenshot of a Discord call I had recently so obviously displays the difference in access between us in Johannesburg, South Africa and those in Zagreb, Croatia. All of the participants in this call have a fibre connection and reasonable quality cameras. It is so obvious that the quality of connection in Zagreb is superior to that in Johannesburg.
