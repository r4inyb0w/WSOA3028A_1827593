15 long stemmed Gerbera daisies. The daisy symbolizes innocence and purity. The Gerbera daisies in particular symbolize happiness. This colourful arrangement will be sure to brighten up any room.

Good Wishes - R42.00

A beautiful bouquet with 16 blue irises, 8 red tulips and 8 pink tulips. The blue irises symbolize hope, the red tulips symbolize love and the pink tulips symbolize well wishes.
